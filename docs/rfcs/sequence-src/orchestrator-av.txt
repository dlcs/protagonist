title DLCS Orchestrator - AV Request

actor Requestor
participant ReverseProxy
participant Orchestrator
participant Source

Requestor->ReverseProxy: /{av-request}
ReverseProxy->Orchestrator: AV requires auth?
Orchestrator-->ReverseProxy: auth reponse

alt requires auth
    ReverseProxy->Orchestrator: /{av-request}
    Orchestrator->Source:read AV (chunks)
    Source-->Orchestrator:AV chunks
    Orchestrator-->Requestor:proxy response in chunks
else open
    ReverseProxy->Source: /{av-request}
    Source-->Requestor: AV asset
end
